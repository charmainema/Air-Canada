Our Workplace Environment

Here at Air Canada we value our employees. Our workplace is structured around community and strength-building among peers. It is always our goal to offer our employees a safe and welcoming environment: one where we can have fun and make memories. We offer our employees a range of team-building activities such as:

  • Sky diving
  • Work for 48hrs straight Marathon Day
  • Paycut Christmas
  • Official employee pen trade
  • Free-for-all paperwork scavenger hunt
  • Free coffee every first Thursday of every second leap year

...and much more!

Our Offices

Take to the skies! Here at Air Canada your office is everywhere! One day you'll be in Canada and the next you might be in Brazil. If the place exists, we fly there! All of our employees are trained to do everything from paperwork to flying our planes, so expect to find yourself in a different office each day.

Our official offices offer a range of amenities such as cubicles, pens, staplers and an occasional chair. (No, we are not going broke, the stuff was just kind of expensive.)
